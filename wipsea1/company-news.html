History: Ecology and Computer Science
WIPSEA idea was born, at Madagascar during summer 2010. Gwénaël Duclos, WIPSEA founder, spent one-month volunteer mission with Cétamada. This association surveys humpback whales in their breeding zone, near Sainte-Marie Island. After an amazing experience with Cétamada, he decided to work to protect the ocean wildlife. After 14 years of professional experience in image processing at Thomson Video Networks, he underwent a vocational training to obtain a Master in Ecology Engineering and Biodiversity Management (Master IEGB) at Montpellier2 University. During his 6 months internship in Murdoch University Cetacean Research Unit, in Western Australia, he began the development of fauna finder software that detects dugongs in images taken from a drone. Then he decided to build his own business in this innovative domain. Now WIPSEA is a reality for the benefit of earth wildlife .
